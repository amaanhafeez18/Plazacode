Printly Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2025

Welcome to Printly. This Privacy Policy explains how Printly (“we”, “our”, or “us”) processes non-sensitive merchant data while providing automated printing services via Clover.

Information We Access

To perform its service, Printly uses OAuth permissions to access merchant order information from Clover. Specifically, we access:

  • Order IDs
  • Order details necessary for printing receipts (e.g., items, totals, tax)
  • Order “source” metadata to filter Ecwid‐online orders

Our Data Handling Policies

  • 🖨️ We only access data necessary to detect and print **Ecwid** orders.
  • 🔒 We **do not store any personal customer data**, beyond order IDs and minimal details required for printing.
  • 🗑️ No order data is retained long‑term. We track order IDs temporarily **in memory** to prevent duplicate printing.
  • 🚫 We do **not send marketing emails**, ads, or affiliate messages.
  • No third parties receive your data from us.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date. Continued use of Printly constitutes acceptance.
